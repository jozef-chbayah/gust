Gusto Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Bacon-Wrapped Dates

    Sweet dates stuffed with almonds or cheese, wrapped in crispy bacon. The combination of sweet, salty, and savory is irresistible.

  • Crispy Calamari

    Tender rings of squid that are breaded and fried until crispy, often served with a side of marinara sauce or lemon wedges.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Shrimp Scampi

    Shrimp sautéed in a garlic, white wine, and butter sauce, typically served over pasta or with a side of crusty bread.

  • Margherita Pizza

    A classic pizza topped with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Tiramisu

    An Italian classic made with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, cocoa powder, and a touch of liqueur. It’s rich and flavorful with a hint of espresso.

  • Crepes Suzette

    Thin crepes served with a sauce made from orange juice, zest, butter, sugar, and sometimes flambéed with orange liqueur.
